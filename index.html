About
Welcome to CancerConnect, a place where you can learn about cancer and connect with people.


Why?
Childhood cancer survivors 2x as likely to suffer chronic health conditions.
47 children are diagnosed with cancer
in the US alone everyday = +17,000 each year..


Mission
Cancer Connect is our solution to the lack of digital communities for cancer patients. Our vision is to bring everyone together in a safe environment where they can interact with other children around the world and learn more about their cancer.
